My Gallery

Explore my collection of contemporary abstract paintings, each piece carefully crafted to evoke emotion and provide a moment of contemplation through the sophisticated use of blue and gray tones.

Azure Dreams

A dynamic abstract composition that captures the essence of flowing water and sky. The interplay of deep blues and soft grays creates a sense of movement and tranquility, inviting viewers to lose themselves in the flowing forms.

Serene Horizons

Medium: Mixed Media on Canvas

Dimensions: 48" x 60"

Year: 2024
